For the spring 2015 semester, our practices will take place on Monday and Thursday from 7:00 – 9:00 PM. Monday practices will occur in the Shockoe Room; Thursday practices will occur in the Canal Room. Both rooms are located on the 2nd floor of the University Student Commons and can be found by taking a left at the top of the main stairwell and then looking right once you've gone through the double doors.
